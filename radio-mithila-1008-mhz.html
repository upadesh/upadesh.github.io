Radio Mithila - ... अपन रेडियो

Radio Mithila 100.8 MHz is one of the most popular FM stations in the country. It’s the first regional FM station that started programs in Maithili language. The 1000 watt capacity FM reaches almost everywhere in Mithila region entertaining more than 10 million people of both Nepal and Bihar (India). This is first time in the region that people have got to listen to the programs and songs of their taste in their own language, Radio Mithila has privilege to be operated by highly skilled professionals who have proven track records of working in radio for more than one and half decades in the country and abroad. It's a pleasure to share that listeners of entire Mithila region has shown tremendous response towards Radio Mithila 100.8 MHz. Radio Mithila 100.8 MHz has opened a portal for information, communication and entertainment in the region. It has two well equipped studios that are fully computerized sending quality sound to its listeners. Equipped with the machines manufactured in the USA, Spain, UK and Germany, Radio Mithila airs news bulletins every hour a day along with rebroadcast of BBC Nepali and Hindi services. We believe that our local investment in technology helps increase the quality of broadcast and coverage in each of our local markets. Radio Mithila 100.8 MHz has largest collection of music which is about 150 thousand Maithili, Hindi, Nepali, Bhojpuri songs stored in its computerized library.
