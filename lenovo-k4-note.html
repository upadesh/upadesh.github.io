Lenovo K4 Note

Upadesh Shrestha Monday, August 22, 2016 Android Phone

Lenovo K4 Note is Android v5.1 (Lollipop) based phone with Vibe UI in the top at रु. 24000. It is Dual SIM, GSM+GSM 4G, 3G, 2G enabled device with 3300 mAh battery of Li-Polymer capacity. Lenovo K4 Note has 5.5 inches screen size touch screen and has the resolution of Full HD (1080 x 1920 pixels) with pixel density of 401 ppi. Phone comes with IPS LCD Capacitive Touchscreen, Multi-touch with Corning Gorilla Glass v3 as screen protection.

Lenovo K4 Note is powered by Octa core, 1.3 GHz, Cortex A53 MediaTek MT6753 64 bit processor with 3 GB of RAM. The phone packs 16 GB of internal storage that can be expanded up to 128 GB via a microSD card.

Lenovo K4 Note has 13 MP main camera with features like Exposure compensation, ISO control, Continuos Shooting, High Dynamic Range mode (HDR) and 5 MP front camera with Fixed Focus.

Lenovo K4 Note measures 153.6 (H) x 76.5 (W) x 9.15 (T) mm and weighs 160 grams. It has connectivity options of Wi-Fi 802.11, a/ac/b/g/n, Bluetooth v4.0 and microUSB 2.0. It has multimedia features of Audio Jack 3.5 mm, Dolby Atmos and sensors in the phone include Fingerprint Sensor, Light sensor, Proximity sensor, Accelerometer, Compass, Gyroscope.
