5 Highest Selling Brands of 2015 in Nepal

Upadesh Shrestha Saturday, January 16, 2016

Nepal is one of the newly emerging and growing economies of the world. The country has seen a rise in many different aspects all around such as the infrastructure, technology, communications and many more. Due to these changes, many major brands have a great standing in the Nepali market. More and more people demand the latest products by these brands to have in their possession and value the money they spend on them. Many stores now keep some of the highest selling brandâ€™s products with them and a number of online marketplaces such as Kaymu and Hamro Bazaar stock up on the latest items needed by the people of Nepal, for their easy accessibility.

There are many brands that were dominating the market but the leading ones left the rest far back. Here are some of the names that you can immediately recognize:

  1. Apple:

Everyone knows what Apple is. This company is known to design, manufacture and market mobile communication and media devices, computers, digital music players, software, networking solutions and many more. The major products include the iPhone, iPad, iPod, Mac and Apple TV.

When it comes to the highest selling products, you can see the iPhone. With its launch a few years back, it took the world with a storm. Now every year, the company launches a newer version with higher levels of features for everyone to enjoy. They run on the iOS operating system and have facilities such as Apple Music, iCloud and much more.

  1. Samsung:

Samsung is popularly known as one of the largest multinational electronics giants out there. With its basis in Seoul, South Korea, it has a huge presence in more than a hundred countries worldwide. It is famous all over the world due to its large product portfolio that has products that suit each and every lifestyle. All the items in their range ensure superior quality and high durability.

The Samsung Mobile is considered to be one of a kind as there is a huge demand for it. They offer the latest looks along with the features to match. Every year the brand is in competition with other famous ones to launch best device. Their phones start from the basics and go up to high quality smartphones with the latest Android operating systems. With high stability, good designs and an amazing overall experience, these are the best for anyone.

  1. Casio:

Casio is one of the most famous brands all around the world and Nepal is no exception to this. It is a multinational electronics manufacturing company located in Japan and it offers many products for everyday and special uses. The products include calculators, mobile phones, musical instruments and watches.

When it comes to the highest selling items, the brand offers watches that can be used for active, luxurious or comfortable lifestyles. Originally, they started off with base line models with the simple minimum sum of features and over the years they have added many new additional ones which set them apart from other brands. The Casio Digital and Analogue watches are the two types they sell with the most popular one being the G Shock which is much loved by all men.

  1. Beats Headphones:

Beats headphones and headsets have taken the world by storm. With ultimate superior sound quality and gorgeous colors, they are a most loved item by many people out there. Developed by the rapper and artist, Dr. Dre, they are a hit among the youth especially.

There are different types of headphones available of the brand and each is suited to the varied requirements of people. The Beats by Dr Dre Studio headphones are the most popular ones out there. The highly attractive feature present in them is the noise cancellation element. All these models require high functioning batteries in order to operate. The Beats by Dr Dre Pro are the best option when it comes to in studio usage. These ones come in stainless steel aluminum coverage, giving them an ultimate look

  1. Hewlett Packard:

Hewlett Packard started its business in California and over the period of many years, has established itself in more than 150 countries worldwide. It has a wide range of devices in its portfolio that provide everyday solutions to its users such as a HP printer or notebook.

When it comes to their most selling item, the HP Laptop dominates over anything else. These products are high functioning with the looks to match. Each of their features are made in a way that they can last for a really long time with the proper functioning elements.
