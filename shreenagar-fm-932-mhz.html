Shreenagar FM - देउरालीको सुसेली

Shreenagar FM is a News and Current Affairs radio station of Nepal that provide the local and international true and quality news on time. Its head office is located in Tansen Nepal. You can listen live On-Air transmission on 93.2 frequencies. It was launched 2007 and work under the management of public awareness programs under the law and the permission of HMG.
